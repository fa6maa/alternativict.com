Alternative Energy Types

Solar Energy

Solar energy is energy from the sun that is converted into thermal or electrical energy. It is a clean and renewable source of energy that can be used in a variety of ways.

  • Pros: Clean and renewable, low maintenance, long lifespan
  • Cons: Can be expensive to install, variable availability depending on location

Learn more about solar energy.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is energy from the wind that is converted into electrical energy. It is a clean and renewable source of energy that is becoming increasingly popular around the world.

  • Pros: Clean and renewable, low maintenance, long lifespan
  • Cons: Can be noisy, can impact wildlife, variable availability depending on location

Learn more about wind energy.

Hydropower

Hydropower is energy from the movement of water that is converted into electrical energy. It is a clean and renewable source of energy that has been used for thousands of years.

  • Pros: Clean and renewable, can provide water storage and flood control, long lifespan
  • Cons: Can impact wildlife and habitats, can have high upfront costs, variable availability depending on location

Learn more about hydropower.
